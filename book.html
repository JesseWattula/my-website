Interview With The Vampire

Interview with the Vampire is Anne Rice's 1976 debut gothic horror novel,
the first in The Vampire Chronicles, that reimagines vampires as complex,
relatable characters grappling with existential despair, love, and morality.

He Who Fights with Monsters

He Who Fights with Monsters is a highly popular LitRPG and progression fantasy novel
series by Travis Deverell (also known as Shirtaloon). It follows Jason Asano, a witty, laid-
back Australian who wakes up in a magical world after his death, where he must fight monsters.
